Peregrine Falcon Red Panda

The Red Panda is not your average panda! It's genetics are more like a racoon than anything else! A Red Panda eats bamboo for most of it's life, but is also a carnivore.

While it's not related to the Giant Panda, (the black and white kind), it is called the Original Panda because it was the first panda to be called panda and gain it's fame.
